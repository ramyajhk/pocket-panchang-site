1. Introduction

Welcome to Pocket Panchang ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Vedic astrology companion application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Important: Pocket Panchang does not require user accounts or login. We do not track individual users or maintain personal profiles. This policy covers the limited data we collect to provide our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data (Optional)

We may request access to your device's location to provide:

  • Accurate local sunrise and sunset times
  • Precise regional Panchang calculations
  • Time zone-specific planetary hours (Hora)
  • Location-based festival and observance dates

Note: Location access is entirely optional. You can use the app by manually entering your city or coordinates.

2.2 Analytics Data (TelemetryDeck)

We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous usage analytics including:

  • App crashes and performance metrics
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and session duration

TelemetryDeck is designed with privacy in mind and does not track individual users or create user profiles.

2.3 Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

We display advertisements through Google AdMob, which may collect:

  • Device identifiers for ad serving
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Device and network information

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings.

2.4 Technical Data (Appwrite Backend)

Our Appwrite backend may automatically collect:

  • IP addresses (for rate limiting and security)
  • Request timestamps and response codes
  • Error logs and crash reports
